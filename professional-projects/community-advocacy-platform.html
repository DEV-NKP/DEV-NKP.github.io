01 / Challenge

The Problem

A leading organization dedicated to advancing racial equity within the Jewish community faced significant operational challenges in managing their complex grantmaking, research initiatives, and community engagement programs. Their existing systems lacked the capability to handle sophisticated grant workflows, effectively store and manage research data, or support their growing community outreach efforts. The organization's website failed to provide engaging user experiences for grant applicants, researchers, and community members. Additionally, sensitive grant information including financial details and applicant data lacked proper security measures, raising significant privacy and compliance concerns that could impact stakeholder trust and program effectiveness.

02 / Solution

Salesforce Solution

As a Salesforce developer, I engineered a comprehensive digital platform to support their advocacy and grantmaking mission through integrated Salesforce solutions with specialized features for racial equity initiatives.

01

Community Portal Development

Implemented Salesforce Experience Cloud to create an interactive, accessible platform featuring research libraries, grant application forms, and community networking tools. The portal provides role-based access for grant applicants, researchers, community members, and staff administrators with specialized content for racial equity resources.

02

Grant Management System with NPSP

Customized Salesforce Nonprofit Success Pack (NPSP) to establish a robust grant lifecycle management system. The solution streamlines grant application processes, approval workflows, fund tracking, and reporting requirements specific to their racial equity initiatives with custom objects and automation.

03

Research Data Management

Developed comprehensive data architecture using Salesforce Sales Cloud and custom objects to effectively store, organize, and analyze research data including survey responses, interview data, and community feedback from their racial equity studies with advanced reporting and analytics capabilities.

04

Process Automation

Built sophisticated Salesforce Flows to automate grant review processes, data collection workflows, and stakeholder notifications. The system automatically routes applications through review stages, triggers approval requests, and notifies staff at critical decision points with audit trails.

05

Document Generation

Integrated PDF Butler to automate the creation of customized grant agreements, approval letters, research reports, and community communications, ensuring consistent branding and reducing manual document preparation time with template management.

06

Secure Public Forms

Developed secure public forms using Experience Cloud and Apex with integrated verification systems to validate applicant information, prevent duplicate submissions, and ensure data integrity throughout the grant application process with security validation.

03 / My Role

My Contribution

I served as the lead Salesforce developer responsible for the complete system architecture and implementation. My role encompassed designing the Experience Cloud portal structure, configuring NPSP for nonprofit grant management, developing custom Apex logic for research data processing, implementing Salesforce Flow automations, establishing PDF Butler integration, creating secure public forms with verification systems, and configuring comprehensive security protocols to protect sensitive grant and research information while ensuring compliance with data protection regulations.

07 / Impact

Outcomes & Impact

The implemented solution delivered transformative improvements in grant processing efficiency, community engagement, and data security, significantly enhancing the organization's ability to advance racial equity initiatives.

Grant Processing Efficiency

Automated workflows and streamlined processes accelerated grant processing time by 40%, enabling the organization to respond more quickly to community needs and distribute funding more efficiently. Grant application completion rates improved by 35% with the user-friendly interface.

Community Engagement Enhancement

The redesigned Experience Cloud portal increased user interaction and engagement by 30%, providing better access to resources, research findings, and grant opportunities for community members and applicants. Community participation in programs increased by 25%.

Data Security and Trust

Implementation of comprehensive security measures including field-level security, encryption, and verification systems built confidence among stakeholders regarding the protection of sensitive grant and research data, strengthening trust in the organization's programs and initiatives with 100% compliance achieved.
