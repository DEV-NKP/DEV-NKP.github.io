01 / Challenge

The Problem

A major workplace safety organization overseeing 1,800+ Ready-Made Garment factories in Bangladesh faced significant operational challenges in managing their comprehensive safety inspection programs. The organization relied on manual processes for tracking structural, electrical, fire, and boiler safety inspections, creating bottlenecks in data collection and reporting. Their existing system lacked efficiency in handling occupational safety complaints, managing remediation programs, and coordinating safety training initiatives across multiple factory locations. Without a centralized platform, the organization struggled to ensure transparency in safety compliance, track corrective action plans effectively, or provide real-time insights to stakeholders including government agencies, global brands, and trade unions. These operational inefficiencies directly impacted worker safety and regulatory compliance in a critical industrial sector.

02 / Solution

Salesforce Solution

As a Salesforce developer, I engineered a comprehensive workplace safety management platform to streamline their safety inspection and compliance operations through integrated Salesforce solutions with specialized features for large-scale factory inspections and offline data collection.

01

Factory and Inspector Portal Development

Implemented Salesforce Experience Cloud to create dedicated portals for factory management and safety inspectors, enabling real-time access to safety resources, inspection schedules, and complaint filing capabilities. The platform provides role-based access controls for different stakeholders including factory management, safety inspectors, and regulatory authorities with multilingual support.

02

Safety Inspection Management

Configured Salesforce Service Cloud to establish a robust system for managing safety inspections across multiple departments including Structural Safety, Electrical Safety, Fire & Life Safety, and Boiler Safety. The solution includes automated inspection scheduling, finding documentation, and corrective action plan tracking with comprehensive reporting and analytics.

03

Offline-Online Data Synchronization

Developed custom Lightning Web Components (LWC) with offline capabilities to enable inspectors to collect safety data in factory environments with limited internet connectivity. The system automatically synchronizes data when online connectivity is restored, ensuring no data loss during field inspections with conflict resolution mechanisms.

04

Document Management

Integrated PDF Butler to automate the generation of inspection reports, safety compliance certificates, and corrective action plans, ensuring consistent formatting and professional documentation across all safety departments with template management and automated distribution.

05

Payment Processing

Implemented Stripe integration for handling inspection fees, training program payments, and certification costs, providing secure financial transaction capabilities within the safety management ecosystem with automated invoicing and receipt generation.

06

Custom Application Development

Built a custom Salesforce application specifically designed for workplace safety management, including custom objects for safety inspections, remediation tracking, training programs, and complaint management tailored to the RMG industry requirements with specialized workflows and approval processes.

07

Offline File and Data Storage

Implemented offline file storage capabilities to enable inspectors to store and access safety documentation, inspection photos, and compliance records without constant internet connectivity, with automatic synchronization to the central Salesforce platform when online with version control and data integrity checks.

03 / My Role

My Contribution

I served as the lead Salesforce developer responsible for the complete workplace safety management system architecture and implementation. My role encompassed designing the Experience Cloud portal structure for factories and inspectors, configuring Service Cloud for safety inspection and complaint management, developing custom LWC components with offline-online synchronization capabilities, implementing PDF Butler integration for automated document generation, establishing Stripe payment processing, building custom safety management applications, and creating offline file storage solutions with online synchronization capabilities while ensuring system scalability and reliability for large-scale operations.

The implemented solution delivered transformative improvements in inspection completion rates, operational efficiency, and stakeholder collaboration, significantly enhancing workplace safety across 1,800+ factories while ensuring regulatory compliance and transparency.
